Objective

Fate of the Kingdom is a randomly generated adventure game where you are given a series of choices on your way to fight the Horned Skull King. Different options will affect your health and power. At the end of your journey, you'll need 25 power in order to kill the Horned Skull King. Otherwise you wil fall as all the others have before you.
